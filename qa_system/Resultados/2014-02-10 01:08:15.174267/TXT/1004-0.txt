Berlin-based Delivery Hero Holding GmbH is an online food-ordering service operating in 14 countries: Sweden, UK, Australia, Finland, Poland, Switzerland, Germany, Austria, Denmark, Russia, Mexico, India, Korea and China. The Delivery Hero platform has over 55,000 restaurants across its worldwide network.takeaway food free of an ordering surcharge for the customers. Meal payments can be made by credit card, PayPal service or with cash on delivery. Delivery Hero services are also accessible via mobile app (iPhone, Android, Windows Phone) among others in Germany, UK, Austria, Poland and Switzerland.
