Tando Adam (Urdu: ٹنڈو آدم‎) (Sindhi: ٽنڊو آدم) is a town in Sindh, Pakistan. The city is also called Daran Jo Shehar (درن جو شهر), because it had eight gates in different places. It was in the Nawab Shah District (now known as Shaheed Benazir Abad District) until 1955, after which it was in the Sanghar District. This city was founded by Mir Adam Khan Mari. In Balochi, Tando means "cantonment".
