This is a list of characters from the visual novel series, Alice in the Country of Hearts, and its sequels and associated media adaptations by developer QuinRose. Most characters in Alice in the Country of Hearts are very loosely based on the original characters from Lewis Carroll's Alice's Adventures in Wonderland and Through the Looking Glass, though there are also a few invented by QuinRose. Characters from this list are primarily listed by the location in which they first appeared in the series.
